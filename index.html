About Viryd

I started Viryd because I got tired of reading solar and heat pump articles that were obviously written to sell something. Every "best solar panel" list ended with a lead form. Every heat pump article was sponsored by an installer.

So I do the research myself. I dig into spec sheets, utility rate structures, and real performance data. When the numbers say something is not worth it, I say so. When a cheaper option outperforms the premium one, I point that out too.

These are independent, data-driven guides. No affiliate links influencing recommendations. No sponsored content. Just someone who has done the homework and wants to save you the trouble.
